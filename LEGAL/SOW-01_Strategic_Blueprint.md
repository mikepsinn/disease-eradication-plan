## 3. SCOPE OF WORK & DELIVERABLES

The Contractor shall perform the services required to conduct foundational research and produce a comprehensive strategic, economic, and legal blueprint. The objective is to de-risk the project and provide a clear, evidence-based roadmap for subsequent development phases.

*   **Key Deliverables:**
    1.  **Health & Economic Impact Analysis:** A formal report modeling the platform's potential to generate Quality-Adjusted Life Years (QALYs) and reduce healthcare expenditures.
    2.  **Economic & Governance Model:** A detailed model for the platform's sustainable economy, including mechanics for the Dynamic Voucher System and a clinical trial insurance framework.
    3.  **Legal, Regulatory & Treaty Framework Analysis:** A thorough educational research analysis covering the global legal landscape (FDA, GDPR, etc.) and international treaty law.
    4.  **Technical Architecture & Development Roadmap:** A high-level technical architecture document and a detailed, phased roadmap for the subsequent development SOWs.

*   **Exclusions:** This SOW is strictly for research, analysis, and planning. No software development, prototyping, lobbying, political activity, or clinical research is authorized under this SOW.
