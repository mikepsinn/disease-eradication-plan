## 3. SCOPE OF WORK & DELIVERABLES

The Contractor shall perform the services required to conduct foundational research and produce a comprehensive strategic, economic, and legal blueprint. The objective is to de-risk the project and provide a clear, evidence-based roadmap for subsequent development phases.

*   **Key Deliverables:**
    1.  **Health & Economic Impact Analysis:** A formal report modeling the platform's potential to generate Quality-Adjusted Life Years (QALYs) and reduce healthcare expenditures.
    2.  **Economic & Governance Model:** A detailed model for the platform's sustainable economy, including mechanics for the Dynamic Voucher System and a clinical trial insurance framework.
    3.  **Legal, Regulatory & Treaty Framework Analysis:** A thorough educational research analysis covering the global legal landscape (FDA, GDPR, etc.) and international treaty law.
    4.  **Technical Architecture & Development Roadmap:** A high-level technical architecture document and a detailed, phased roadmap for the subsequent development SOWs.

*   **Exclusions:** This SOW is strictly for research, analysis, and planning. No software development, prototyping, lobbying, political activity, or clinical research is authorized under this SOW.

## 4. GRANT FUNDING & PAYMENT SCHEDULE

The Foundation will provide a total grant of **$120,000** for the successful completion of the services outlined in this SOW.

*   **Budget Justification:** The grant amount is allocated to the following workstreams:
    *   **Specialized Legal & Economic Analysis:** ~$80,000
    *   **Strategic & Technical Leadership:** ~$30,000
    *   **Administrative & Operational Support:** ~$10,000

*   **Payment Schedule:** Payments will be made upon the acceptance of specific deliverables, with amounts corresponding to the value and effort of each milestone, structured as follows:
    1.  **$10,000** upon execution of this SOW.
    2.  **$20,000** upon acceptance of the draft Health & Economic Impact Analysis (QALY model).
    3.  **$10,000** upon acceptance of the final Health & Economic Impact Analysis report.
    4.  **$20,000** upon acceptance of the draft Economic & Governance Model (Voucher System).
    5.  **$10,000** upon acceptance of the final Economic & Governance Model, including the insurance framework.
    6.  **$25,000** upon acceptance of the Legal, Regulatory & Treaty Framework Analysis report.
    7.  **$10,000** upon acceptance of the high-level Technical Architecture document.
    8.  **$15,000** upon acceptance of the final, detailed Development Roadmap for subsequent SOWs.
