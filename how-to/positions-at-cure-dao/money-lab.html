Money Lab

  • Investments are essential for any organization. Naturally, we want to ensure that CureDAO's investment initiatives are perfectly aligned with our mission and vision

 

Benefits

A share of 10% of the total outstanding CureDAO tokens, proportional to your hourly rate and number of hours invested.

Based on comparable health DAO launches, this would likely lead to far greater compensation than your standard rate.

Other benefits include:

  • Partake in governance decisions
  • Flexible remote work hours

 

Responsibilities

  • The role includes collaborating with lab members and consultants on a wide variety of investment-related tasks
  • The lab monitors the characteristics of our community, optimizes the investment opportunities, and aligns them with CureDAO's mission and vision

 

Qualifications

  • Strong willingness to learn and grow
  • Great pitching skills
  • Excellent at establishing, improving, and sustaining mutually beneficial relationships
  • Great problem solver
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Passionate about our vision
