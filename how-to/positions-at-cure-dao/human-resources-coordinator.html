Human Resources Coordinator

  • CureDAO is helping create personalized precision medicine of the future by establishing an open-source platform for health data
  • The HR work is essential for any DAO. Naturally, we want to ensure that CureDAO's HR initiatives are perfectly aligned with our mission and vision

 

Benefits

A share of 10% of the total outstanding CureDAO tokens, proportional to your hourly rate and number of hours invested.

Based on comparable health DAO launches, this would likely lead to far greater compensation than your standard rate.

Other benefits include:

  • Partake in governance decisions
  • Flexible remote work hours

 

Responsibilities

  • The HR Lab coordinator is responsible for the HR work of CureDAO's community and operations
  • The role includes collaborating with lab members and consultants on a wide variety of HR quests
  • The lab monitors the characteristics of our community, optimizes the HR flow, and aligns them with CureDAO's mission and vision

 

Qualifications

  • Strong willingness to learn and grow
  • Good HR skills
  • Great problem solver
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Passionate about our vision
